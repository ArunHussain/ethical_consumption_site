About Us

Our Mission

Helping you make decisions

We believe everyone has the right to consume from companies whose ethics they support. Use our site to find up to date information on the practices of companies so that no matter your personal morals, you can discover if companies align with them!

How To Use Our Site

Get information about many companies

Search for a company using the search bar at the top or explore using the categories at the top. To search by product type, make an account and answer our preference questions. Then, when you search at the top, a list of companies matching your values which sell that product will also be shown!
